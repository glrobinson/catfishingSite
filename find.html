Common “decision rules”

  • Clean + dry? More likely recycle.
  • Greasy or food-stained? Often trash/compost.
  • Film plastic? Usually not curbside.
  • Hazardous/electronic? Special drop-off.

Why local rules matter

Cities accept different plastics, glass rules vary, and composting depends on local services. But it is best to look at your city/campus guidelines.
