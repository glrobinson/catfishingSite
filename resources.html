Quick cheat sheet

These are common defaults, your local program may differ, especially for plastics.

Recycle
  • Metal cans (empty, quick rinse)
  • Clean paper + cardboard
  • Glass jars/bottles (often)
  • Rigid plastics (varies)
Compost
  • Food scraps
  • Soiled paper (often)
  • Yard waste (if accepted)
Trash
  • Plastic bags/wrap (often)
  • Dirty/greasy paper products
  • Mixed materials you can’t separate
